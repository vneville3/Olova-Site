## Current state
- The site is structurally built but still needs final architectural alignment.

## Core strategic summary
- OLOVA is mainly a founder-led design and advocacy platform.
- Product is now a secondary or tertiary lane.
- Community builds trust.
- Design builds authority.
- Trade/product builds optionality.

## Primary goal of the site
- Attract and convince the right people to involve Vaughan in real skatepark, plaza, youth-space, or advocacy-related projects.

## Audience priority
1. Project decision-makers and collaborators
2. Core skaters and advocates
3. Product or trade-related visitors

## Recommended site structure
- Home
- Design
- Community
- Stories
- Trade
- Contact

## Page roles
### Home
- Orient and route
- Establish Vaughan quickly
- Show Design and Belong as proof

### Design
- Primary authority page
- Strong visuals and clear thinking

### Community
- Belong and real-world involvement
- Trust and proof

### Stories
- Biography, philosophy, history, proof archive
- Human layer of the site

### Trade
- Small, honest product and future import lane

### Contact
- Direct and simple

## Critical reminders
- Do not let product dominate the identity
- Do not equalize all lanes
- Do not drift back into internal planning language
- Do not overclaim scale
- Do not build for everyone at once
