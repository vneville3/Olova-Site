What is OLOVA?

OLOVA is a registered trading company at an early stage of development. The business is being built around supplier access, product evaluation, and selective introduction of opportunities into the Canadian market. The direction is intentionally flexible at this stage. The goal is not to overpromise, but to build real capability and move when the right opportunities are ready.

Sourcing

Supplier-side groundwork

OLOVA is developing the relationships and communication channels needed to identify viable product opportunities from abroad and assess their fit for the Canadian market.

Market-side judgment

The focus is on practical opportunities, not noise. Products need a clear reason to exist, a credible path to market, and enough margin or utility to justify attention.
