Wider body of work

Not every project here is equally finished. That is part of the point.

This page keeps the broader archive visible without pretending every project belongs in the lead slot. Some are concept support, some are proposal work, some are more complete, and some are here because the idea inside them still matters.

The through-line is consistent: public space designed well enough that skating belongs there on purpose, not as an afterthought.
